About me

Hey, I'm Jenny and I run Pawsome Strolls! I have loved dogs my whole life and so it was a no-brainer to start up a business that is centred around caring for dogs. I started off as a teen looking after family and friends' pups, popping in to give them a fuss or take them out and throw a ball after school or at the weekends. I decided to study Canine Behaviour theory at college and in 2013 I graduated with a level 4 Diploma. Ever since then I've worked with various dogs from all backgrounds including a year out in Thailand working with charities to care for street dogs

I'm delighted to offer you my services in caring for your pet. From popping in during the day for a visit when you are at work, taking them out for a long and enriching walk or even having your dog over whilst you are away on holiday!

Check out the services section for more information about prices and discounts. You can also have a browse through the testimonials to see the pawsome times I've had with some wonderful dogs already.
